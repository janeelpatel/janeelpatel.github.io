About Me

Hello! I am Janeel Patel, a second-year undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. I am pursuing a dual degree (B. Tech with M. Tech) in Electrical Engineering with specialisation in Communication and Signal Processing.
Based on what I have explored so far, I am interested in Digital Electronics, Analog Circuits and Deep Learning. I am particularly intrigued by Microprocessors and Embedded Systems and would love to learn more about these topics.

I love to listen to music with pop rock being my favourite genre. I also like to swim and play football.
